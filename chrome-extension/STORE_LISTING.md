## Extension Name
Connect with LiveSchool

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Quick access to your LiveSchool scheduling links. Copy booking URLs instantly without opening the dashboard.

## Detailed Description
Connect with LiveSchool gives you instant access to all your scheduling links right from your browser toolbar.

FEATURES:
• See all your active events in one popup
• One-click copy for any booking link
• Search to quickly find specific events
• View upcoming slot counts at a glance
• Works with individual and team event types

PERFECT FOR:
• Customer Success teams sharing booking links in emails
• Support agents scheduling calls with customers
• Anyone who shares LiveSchool links frequently

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Install the extension
2. Connect your LiveSchool account with your personal token
3. Click the icon to see all your events
4. Copy or open any booking link instantly

REQUIREMENTS:
• A LiveSchool account at liveschoolhelp.com
• Your personal Quick Links token (found in Settings)

PRIVACY:
• Your token is stored locally in your browser
• We only fetch your event data from LiveSchool
• No data is shared with third parties
• Full privacy policy: https://liveschoolhelp.com/privacy/chrome-extension
